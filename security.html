Security

Your data remains under your complete control

Explorito is built with privacy at its core. Everything stays on your device - we never access, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers.

Local-Only data processing
All your files, emails, and app data are processed and indexed locally on your own device. Nothing is ever uploaded to the cloud or stored on external servers. Your information never leaves your control.
Enterprise-grade authentication
We implement OAuth 2.0 authentication protocols for secure connections to your third-party applications. This means direct, encrypted communication between your device and services like Google, Microsoft, and Slack - with no intermediary access.
End-to-End Encryption
All data synchronization uses SSL/TLS encryption during transit. When connecting to external services, your information is fully encrypted from your device to the destination, ensuring complete protection during data transfer.
Zero-knowledge framework
Explorito operates on a zero-knowledge principle. Our team cannot and will never access your data. Everything remains on your device within your user profile, isolated from other users and completely invisible to our systems.
